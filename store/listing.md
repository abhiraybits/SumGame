## App name

## Short description (max 80 chars)
Freeze numbers on a 5×5 grid and match pairs that add up to 10!

## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Can you keep up with the numbers?

Sum 10 is a fast-paced number puzzle game. Numbers slide in from every edge of a 5×5 grid — tap to freeze one, then wait for a moving number to collide with it. If they add up to 10, you score a point!

Simple to learn, impossible to master.

🎮 THREE GAME MODES

⚔️ Survival
Score as many matches as you can before you lose all 3 lives. The game speeds up over time — how long can you last?

🗺️ Campaign
Clear every cell on the board to advance to the next stage. Each stage is faster and more challenging than the last.

🏀 Bouncy
Numbers don't just disappear when they reach the edge — they bounce back up to 3 times, giving you more chances to freeze and match.

✨ FEATURES
• Satisfying particle burst effects on every match
• Sound feedback — a pleasing ding on success, a thud on miss
• Speed multiplier setting to customize the challenge
• Unlimited lives mode for casual play
• Clean dark UI that's easy on the eyes

🧠 HOW TO PLAY
1. Numbers slide across the grid automatically
2. Tap any cell to FREEZE its number (it glows blue)
3. When a moving number hits your frozen cell — if they sum to 10, you score!
4. Wrong sum? You lose a life. Three misses and it's game over.

Pairs that work: 1+9, 2+8, 3+7, 4+6, 5+5, 0+10
